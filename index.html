    About Us

    The revolutionary team is basically a group of intellectual student who have set themselves aside and taken upon themselves the initiative to effect changes in all aspect of the student life. They do this through writing, publishing speaking and all grounds work involved to implement the change they stand for. The core motive and itention of the revolutionary team is to seek and to be the frontliners in all activities that wil bring liberation and meet the interest of the student populace.

    Our Mission is to set strutures and systems to monitor and guide SRC activities practically in emitting the change we stand for to ensure Transparency, Respect and Efficiency with desired results.
